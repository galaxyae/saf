3RD Party Logistics

Safzon Group 3PL Division provides diamond, jewellery and watch companies a comprehensive package of third party logistical solutions. The competitive cost-per-unit structure ensures companies only pay for what they use, saving them time, money and worry.

  • Distribution

    • Varied shipping methods to support any destination worldwide

  • System Management

    • Online web application to support full transparency and inventory management.

  • Bonded and FTZ Vault Facilities

  • Batch and Traceability

    • Real time updates on product status.

  • Pick and Pack

    • Comprehensive operation that fulfils each and every step of the product line.
